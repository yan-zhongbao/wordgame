## Character Reference

**Bobo** (主角)
- Age 10, Grade 4, boarding school, Chengdu
- Favourite colour: red; also loves rainbows
- Likes: Math, IT class, running (track club), Lego club
- Dislikes: Chinese class (too much writing; writes slowly)
- Food: pasta, white rice, banana, orange, coconut; dislikes whole wheat bread
- Hobby: military drawings (soldiers, armies, tanks, planes, warships on blank paper when bored)
- Likes: superhero movies (Spider-Man etc.), military museums
- Best friend: Pengpeng — video games, bike rides, sandbox, travel together

**Family**
- Sister: in high school
- Paternal aunt's son (表哥): same year as sister
- Maternal uncle's daughter (表妹): age 6
- 爷爷奶奶: 5h train + 2h drive, or 8h self-drive
- 外公外婆 + maternal uncle: far away, need plane
- Parents: prefer cotton clothes; mom insists on whole wheat bread

**Travel history**: USA, UK, Italy, France, Singapore, Maldives, Australia, Egypt (desert + pyramids + camel)

**School**: Teacher Yang (wears earrings); class has 3 pairs of twins

**Story hooks**:
- Border fingerprint always fails → manual lane
- Puts bag on front abroad to prevent theft
- Used to trade toys at school → teacher banned it
- Attended great-grandma's funeral, saw coffin buried
- Traditional costume exhibition held at school (age 10)

## Writing Rules

- Elementary level only: short, simple sentences. No complex subordinate clauses.
- Only use words from wordlist.txt. Do NOT introduce new vocabulary.
- Seasonal flow for all stories: Autumn → Winter → Spring → Summer
- My City Chapter 4 uses past tense (earthquake museum, 2008 events).
- Each chapter: ~400–600 words, readable in one sitting.
